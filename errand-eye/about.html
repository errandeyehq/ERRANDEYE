About Errand Eye

Built on one idea: your time is worth protecting.

About Us

We redefine convenience by bridging the gap between time, trust, and tasks.

At Errand Eye, we are a multi-service brand rooted in Tours & Travel, Hospitality Support, and Logistics. We serve individuals and businesses that need reliability, professionalism, and solutions tailored to how they work.

What began as a simple vision, to make everyday responsibilities easier, has grown into a reputable brand serving corporates, international clients, tourists, busy professionals, and solo travelers across Kenya, Nigeria, South Africa, and beyond.

Our approach is simple but powerful: listen, customize, and deliver. From crafting seamless travel itineraries and managing guest services, to handling errands and logistical operations with precision, Errand Eye steps in when excellence is not optional.

We are more than a service provider; we are an extension of your priorities. With a deep understanding of customer needs and a commitment to excellence, we live our promise: “Any task, just ask.”

Problem Statement

There is a clear need for a one-stop, customer-first solution.

In today’s fast-paced world, individuals and organizations face growing pressure to manage multiple responsibilities with limited time and resources. From planning travel experiences to managing hospitality operations and coordinating time-sensitive logistics, many providers still fall short in delivering consistent quality, flexibility, or personal attention.

Corporates often struggle with fragmented service providers who lack the capacity for tailored travel and logistics support. Tourists, especially international or solo travelers, find navigating unfamiliar environments difficult without trusted local guidance. Meanwhile, busy professionals and expatriates are overwhelmed by daily errands, administrative demands, and coordination challenges.

Errand Eye was created to fill this gap: offering end-to-end, integrated solutions in tours & travel, hospitality support, and logistics, all under one reliable, accessible brand.
