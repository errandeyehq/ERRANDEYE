Tours & Travel

Journeys built around how you actually want to move.

Every itinerary starts with a conversation, not a template. We map out where you're going, how much time you actually have between meetings or flights, and the pace that keeps a trip enjoyable instead of exhausting. From weekend getaways along the coast to multi-city circuits spanning Kenya, Nigeria, and South Africa, our travel desk builds the route, books the stays, and lines up the excursions so nothing collides with anything else on your calendar.

We work with vetted local guides and operators who know the terrain and the small details that turn a good trip into a memorable one, whether that's a sunrise game drive or a quiet afternoon at a site most guidebooks miss. If plans shift mid-trip, a delayed flight, a closed road, a change of heart, we adjust bookings in real time. Payment is arranged only after you approve the full plan, with every quote itemized upfront.

  • • Custom itineraries built around your actual schedule
  • • Vetted local guides and excursion operators
  • • Real-time rebooking if plans change mid-trip
  • • Coverage across Kenya, Nigeria, and South Africa
  • • Itemized, pre-approved quotes with no surprise costs

Hospitality Support

Guest experiences handled before your guests notice a gap.

Hospitality support covers everything that happens between booking a room and actually feeling welcome in it. We secure accommodation and restaurant reservations that match the occasion, negotiate on availability during peak periods, and confirm details directly with venues so nothing is left to a booking engine's best guess.

For visiting executives, family events, or milestone celebrations, we coordinate welcome arrangements, dietary requirements, and special requests in advance. When something goes sideways at check-in, an overbooked hotel, a missed dietary note, we're already on the phone resolving it before the guest has to raise it. We also handle the smaller touches: welcome gifts, transport between venues, and briefing on-the-ground contacts.

  • • Direct-to-venue reservation confirmation, not just booking-engine holds
  • • Dietary, accessibility, and special-occasion coordination
  • • On-the-ground issue resolution before guests notice a problem
  • • Welcome gifts and arrival touches arranged in advance
  • • Multi-stop hosting coordination for visiting groups

Airport Transfers

Pickups timed to your flight, not the clock.

Airport transfers sound simple until a flight lands two hours late and there's no one tracking it. We monitor live flight status for every pickup, so drivers arrive when you actually land, not when the original schedule said you would. Vehicles are matched to the trip, executive cars for business travel, larger vehicles for families with luggage.

Meet-and-greet service is available at arrivals for travelers who'd rather not navigate an unfamiliar terminal alone. Departures get the same precision: pickup times are calculated backward from check-in and security windows, with buffer built in for traffic. For recurring travelers, we set up standing arrangements so bookings don't need re-confirming trip by trip.

  • • Live flight tracking for accurate pickup timing
  • • Meet-and-greet service at arrivals
  • • Vehicle selection matched to trip type and group size
  • • Departure timing calculated for traffic and security windows
  • • Standing arrangements available for recurring travelers

Visa Assistance

Documentation handled with someone checking the fine print.

Visa and travel document requirements change often and vary by nationality, destination, and purpose of travel, and getting a detail wrong can mean a rejected application or a missed departure date. We review what's actually required, gather supporting documentation, and complete applications correctly the first time.

Passport renewals are handled with the same care, particularly useful when an expiring passport turns up close to a departure date. If a document is lost or stolen while traveling, we coordinate emergency replacement with the relevant embassy. We also track visa validity windows for frequent travelers, flagging renewals before they become urgent.

  • • Destination-specific visa requirement review
  • • Embassy appointment and application coordination
  • • Expedited passport renewal support
  • • Lost or stolen document emergency assistance
  • • Ongoing visa validity tracking for frequent travelers

Corporate Travel

One coordinated program instead of ten individual bookings.

Corporate travel gets complicated fast once more than one traveler is involved, different itineraries, cost centers, and approval chains, all needing to stay consistent with company policy. We manage travel programs as a single coordinated effort, tracking every traveler's itinerary, accommodation, and transport from one point of contact.

Bookings follow your company's policy on budget, preferred vendors, and approvals, and centralized itinerary management means anyone on your team can get a status update without chasing emails. For conferences, offsites, and client visits, we sequence bookings and flag conflicts before they become problems, with reporting available for finance and compliance teams.

  • • Centralized management across multi-traveler itineraries
  • • Company travel policy and vendor compliance
  • • Conflict detection across overlapping schedules
  • • Consolidated reporting for finance and compliance
  • • One point of contact for the entire travel program

Logistics & Errands

The tasks that eat your day, handled by someone who follows through.

Some tasks don't need a specialist, they just need someone reliable to actually do them and confirm they're done. Logistics and errand support covers courier runs, document pickups and drop-offs, bill payments, and the small obligations that pile up when there's no time to run them yourself.

Requests can be one-off, an urgent document that needs to cross town today, or a standing weekly arrangement for a business. Every task is confirmed on completion, with proof of delivery when relevant, so there's no ambiguity about whether it actually got done. Same-day service is available, and we're upfront about what's realistically achievable in a given window.

  • • Same-day courier and document handling
  • • Bill payments and routine task management
  • • Recurring or standing errand arrangements for businesses
  • • Completion confirmation with proof of delivery
  • • No task minimum, small requests are welcome

Event & Retreat Planning

Group logistics managed so the event feels effortless on the day.

Planning something for a group, a corporate retreat, a family celebration, a team offsite, means coordinating venues, catering, transport, and timing across everyone attending, and a single missed detail can throw off the whole day. We handle venue sourcing based on your group size, budget, and tone, negotiate rates, and confirm every detail directly with the venue.

Group movement is coordinated end to end: arrivals, transport between venues, and departures are scheduled so no one is left waiting around. On-site support is available for events that need someone managing logistics in real time, checking guests in and troubleshooting vendor issues, so the host can actually participate instead of running the event themselves.

  • • Venue sourcing matched to group size and budget
  • • End-to-end group transport and arrival coordination
  • • On-site logistics support during the event
  • • Vendor negotiation and direct venue confirmation
  • • Scaled for both small retreats and large gatherings

Concierge Shopping

Personal sourcing handled with discretion and good judgment.

Concierge shopping is for purchases that need a personal touch, or simply someone else's time: gift sourcing for an occasion that matters, personal shopping for someone who can't get to the shops, or recurring errands easier to hand off than to keep managing. We source gifts based on the occasion, recipient, and budget, handling presentation and delivery so the result feels thoughtful rather than last-minute.

Personal purchases are handled with clear communication throughout, confirming sizing, preferences, and budget before buying anything. Requests involving sensitive or private circumstances are handled with the same discretion we apply across the business. Delivery can go directly to the recipient or back to you, whichever suits the occasion.

  • • Occasion-based gift sourcing and presentation
  • • Personal shopping with confirmed sizing and preferences
  • • Discreet handling of private or sensitive requests
  • • Recurring errand arrangements for busy schedules
  • • Delivery direct to recipient or back to you

Relocation Support

A steadier landing for people arriving somewhere new.

Moving to a new city or country involves a long list of unfamiliar decisions, where to live, how the neighborhoods differ, what the first few weeks actually require, made harder by the fact that you're often making them before you've set foot in the place. We help with housing search support, matching neighborhoods to your budget, commute, and lifestyle, and coordinating viewings so you're not judging a new city from photos alone.

Orientation and setup support covers the practical groundwork of a new base: understanding local services and knowing who to call before you actually need to know. During the first few weeks, when small issues tend to surface, we stay available as a point of contact so new arrivals aren't figuring out an unfamiliar system alone.

  • • Neighborhood and housing search support
  • • Viewing coordination before and after arrival
  • • Practical orientation for local services and essentials
  • • Ongoing point of contact through the first weeks
  • • Support for individuals, families, and relocating teams
