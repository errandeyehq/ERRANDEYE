What We Handle

Nine service lines. One point of contact.

Whatever the request, it moves through the same trusted channel, with no juggling vendors and no chasing updates. You ask once, and Errand Eye coordinates the rest.

Personalized itineraries and curated excursions built around how you actually want to travel, not a fixed package.

  • Excursion & activity booking
  • Local guide coordination

Airport Transfers

  • Centralized billing & reporting

Event & Retreat Planning

Corporate retreats, team offsites, and private events planned end-to-end, from venue sourcing to on-the-ground logistics.

Concierge Shopping & Personal Errands

Personal shopping, gift sourcing, and day-to-day errands handled discreetly on your behalf.
